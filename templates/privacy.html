Attention Footprint

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What this app does

Attention Footprint calculates the collective watch time your videos have generated — how much total human time viewers have spent watching your content.

Data we access

When you connect your TikTok account, we request access to:

  • Your public profile (display name, avatar)
  • Your public video list (titles, durations, view counts)

For YouTube, we access only publicly available channel and video data using your channel handle — no login required.

Data we store

We do not store any of your data. All analysis happens in memory during your session and is discarded immediately after. We do not retain your TikTok access token, video data, or any personal information beyond the duration of your browser session.

Third-party APIs

  • YouTube Data API v3 — subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • TikTok API — subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
